## 1. Executive Summary (Re-scoped)

Independent and mid-sized brokerage firms that specialise in **small-to-medium (≤ 150 k sq ft) single-building or land-use projects inside an existing city plan** lose deals when they cannot quickly prove viability to owners or investors.

Our portal will let licensed U.S. commercial & mixed-use brokers:

1. **Discover** parcels or structures that match basic zoning filters (e.g., C-2, MX-3).  
2. **Instantly generate “City-Scale Snapshot” packs** showing population, foot-traffic, comps, and existing city-plan restrictions inside an adjustable radius (0.5 – 10 km).  
3. **Model ROI & opportunity cost** without spreadsheets, tuned for discrete single-building scenarios (retail pad, infill apartment, light-industrial, etc.).  
4. **Request exclusive listing/development rights** through a structured, auditable workflow aligned with municipal planning rules.

Core differentiation:  
• < 2 hr total due-diligence (vs. 2-4 weeks today) for **city-plan-conforming projects**,  
• embedded financial calculator calibrated for ≤ $50 M gross project value,  
• zoning/land-use overlay that warns when a proposal violates the adopted comprehensive plan.

Target market: 15–150 person brokerage firms in top-50 U.S. MSAs.  
Launch goal: public beta in ≤ 9 months.
